Why Learn From Us?

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this for?

✅ Aspiring VFX artists with a passion for visual storytelling

✅ Students and creators interested in film, games, or digital effects

✅ Designers and professionals looking to switch to a VFX career

Do I need a background in film or design?
Not at all! We start from the fundamentals of VFX and gradually train you to work on professional-grade projects — no prior experience needed.
What software will I learn?
You'll work with top VFX tools like Adobe After Effects, Nuke, Blender, and DaVinci Resolve, used by professionals in the film and entertainment industry.
Who are the instructors?
Industry veterans and certified VFX artists with real studio experience will guide you through every step with mentorship and feedback.
What can I expect after completing the course?
A job-ready VFX showreel, a recognized certificate, and access to career opportunities in films, gaming, advertising, and OTT platforms.
